This 3D scene was made with Aframe (more info here). It is a reimagining of Carmen Piemonte's eye-catching geometric painting which is shown below the AFrame glitch embed. Piemonte (Chile, 1930) is a notable Chilean painter known for her work in the artistic space of "Forma y Espacio," which is a movement that flourished in the 1970s.

The Ralli Santiago Museum in Chile houses much of her work; here is an article published by the museum to highlight their "Kinetic and Geometric" exhibit.
