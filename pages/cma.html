WORK RESUME ABOUT

I am the project lead for the website launch of the Computational Media Ambassadors, meant to serve as a source of inspiration, a project portfolio, and an art gallery for all Computational Media majors at Georgia Tech.

Our grassroots project goal is to create a striking, interactive, and cutting-edge website by employing industry-standard UI/UX Design processes and practices such as ideation, prototyping, usability testing, asset creation, and more. Everything will be documented, for the benefit of future generations of Computational Media Ambassadors. The initial phase is ramping up now and the bulk of the work will occur throughout Spring 2022. I am leading a team of anywhere from 5-15 novice designers, researchers, and developers.

I am regularly consulting with Bits of Good and professional UI/UX designers on this project, in order to guide me along the process of directing a large-scale design project.

Engineering is expected to begin in the Fall 2022. The final product should be complete by Spring 2023.
