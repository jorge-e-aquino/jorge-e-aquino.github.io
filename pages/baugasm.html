WORK FIGMA RESUME ABOUT

My first foray into working with the Creative Cloud culminated around the futuristic and gaudy style of Baugasm, an art style named after the art movement "Bauhaus."

These pieces were initialized in Illustrator using a mesh tool which was then colored and then I exported them into Photoshop for most of the work.

This next one is one of my favorites. I was really excited to watch The Lighthouse and I decided to make a little poster just for the fun of it. I studied up on typography and wanted to make a poster reminiscent of posters from 60's psych rock bands but constrained to a simple structure.

I realized how beautiful human faces look when paired with a complimentary color palette, and being that the film is shot in grayscale, the gradient and vanishing typography really work well together. I really got into the flow of creating posters that I find visually attractive even if they are abstract.
